# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Metaphysical Cartography
### **The Leonora Carrington Edition**

**Style:** **Metaphysical Surrealism.** The aesthetic is characterized by a muted, earthy palette, elongated figures, and a dreamlike, hazy atmosphere that feels like a 16th-century alchemical manuscript. The landscape is not a modern map, but a **symbolic parchment** where geography and mythology merge.

**The Vista:** A vast, bird's-eye view of **[City, Country]**, rendered as an ancient, weathered map. The street grids of **[Landmark 1]** and **[Landmark 2]** are visible, but they are overgrown with strange, bioluminescent moss and silver threads. The landscape appears to be a living entity, with mountains that look like sleeping giants.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, stratospheric **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** hovers in a pale, twilight sky. It is translucent, resembling a ghostly egg or a hollowed-out reliquary. The "Life-Form" interaction is a tall, robed figure with a bird's head performing a ritual inside the ingredient, stirring a cauldron of light.
* **Secondary:** Smaller **[Remaining Ingredients]** drift through the air like floating orbs or oversized moth wings, trailing silver dust.
* **Lighting & Shadows:** Soft, diffuse lighting with no clear sun source. Shadows are long, thin, and take on the shapes of mythical beasts (half-human, half-animal) that prowl the streets below.

**The Palette (Alchemical Sync):**
* The color scheme uses the deep, murky tones of **[Ingredient Colors]**—specifically ochre, sienna, and verdigris—blended with the **[National Colors]** as if they were fading pigments on old vellum.
* **Atmosphere:** The sky is a gradient of smoky lavender and gold, filled with drifting, symbolic stars and astrological sigils.
* **Geographic Feature:** Iconic features like **[River/Sea]** are rendered as flowing liquid mercury or black ink, filled with tiny, swimming human-headed fish.

**The Atmosphere:** Strange, winged hybrid creatures fly among the clouds. **[Texture/Botanical Elements]** grow directly out of the sky as if it were a garden of floating, tangled roots.

**The Text:** Floating in the center of the sky is the phrase: **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"**. The text is written in an elegant, spidery Latin-style script, with golden "illuminated" first letters featuring tiny illustrations of the ingredients.

**The Scale:** Tiny, elongated figures in hooded cloaks wander the map below, some carrying oversized spoons or silver chalices, emphasizing the ritualistic and cosmic nature of the "Flavor Dream."
