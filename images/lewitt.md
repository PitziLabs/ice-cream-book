# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism
### **The Sol LeWitt Edition**

**Style:** A rigorous, minimalist, **procedural graphic illustration** inspired by **Sol LeWitt (Structures and Wall Drawings)**. This requires **ultra-sharp, precise lines** of a single, uniform weight, and an emphasis on pure geometric forms. Texture is absent; all forms are defined by their **modular structure, isometric projection, and mathematical progression.** **The Vista:** A vast, sprawling aerial vista of **[City, Country]**, rendered from the immense scale of a **3D satellite map**, but **drawn entirely as an isometric grid of pure lines.** The landscape is a dense, interlocking field of identical, open 3D cubic frames. Landmarks like **[Landmark 1]** are depicted as chemically optimized, geometric structures. 

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, city-sized **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** hovers high in the stratosphere. It is an **impossible, modular cubic network** formed by hundreds of interlocking, open frames. Its side must be **EXPLODED open by a perfect cubic cavity**, revealing inside a rigid, isometric, cross-hatched diagram of the **[Narrative Moment/Life-Form]**.
* **Secondary:** Large **[Remaining Ingredients]** drift at varying altitudes. These are not spheres, but perfect geometric polyhedrons (e.g., dodecahedrons or pyramids) formed by interlocking lines, containing only a simple, raw, colored shape.
* **Lighting & Shadows:** There is no "lighting." All shadows are rendered as **dense, uniform, parallel cross-hatching**, strictly following the isometric grid rules. They communicate immense scale by the overwhelming repetition of lines falling across the cubic city.

**The Palette (National-Culinary Sync):**
* The color scheme blends **[Ingredient Colors]** and **[National Colors]**. These must be rendered in visceral, flat tones within the isometric grid, strictly adhering to LeWitt's limited primary color usage (Red, Yellow, Blue, White, Black) where possible to represent the culinary/national sync.
* **Atmosphere:** The sky is a flat, mathematically calculated gradient or a rigid grid of varying line density.
* **Geographic Feature:** Iconic features like **[River/Sea]** are rendered as stark, flat geometric planes of pure color interrupting the grid.

**The Atmosphere:** Flying **[Local Vehicle Type]** move among the geometric clouds, rendered as perfect moving prisms. **[Texture/Botanical Elements]** drift through the air like mathematical vectors and floating geometric planes.

**The Text:** The phrase **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"** is integrated into the sky. It is not handwritten; it is rendered as a **perfect, isometric 3D architectural block structure**, adhering strictly to the perspective grid.

**The Scale:** **Tiny, geometric stick-figures** are visible on the grid below. They are identical, modular units, perfectly spaced, emphasizing the immense mathematical scale of the structures above them.
