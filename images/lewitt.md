# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism (v4.0)
### **The Sol LeWitt Edition**

**Style:** A rigorous, minimalist, **procedural graphic illustration** inspired by **Sol LeWitt (Structures and Wall Drawings)**. This requires **ultra-sharp, precise black lines** of a single, uniform weight, and an emphasis on pure geometric forms. Texture is completely absent; all forms are defined by their **modular structure, isometric projection, and mathematical progression.** Colors are limited to LeWitt’s core palette (Red, Yellow, Blue, White, Black).

**The Vista:** A **vast, sprawling aerial vista** of **[City, Country]**, rendered from the immense scale of a **3D satellite map**, but **drawn entirely as an isometric grid of pure lines.** The landscape is not a realistic city, but a dense, interlocking field of identical, open white 3D cubic frames. Landmarks like **Monserrate** and **Torre Colpatria** are depicted as friendly, slightly patched-together but chemically optimized cartoon buildings. The entire vista is built from repeating, identical, white 3D cubic structures.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, city-sized, hyper-detailed **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** hovers high in the stratosphere in a **three-quarters off-center position**. In this style, the primary ingredient is not a physical object, but an **impossible, modular cubic network** formed by hundreds of interlocking, open white-frame cubes. Its side must be **EXPLODED open by a perfect cubic cavity**, revealing inside a rigid, isometric, cross-hatched diagram of the **[Narrative Moment/Life-Form]**.
* **Secondary:** Large **[Remaining Ingredients]** drift at varying altitudes like massive celestial moons. These are not spheres, but perfect geometric polyhedrons (e.g., dodecahedrons or pyramids) formed by interlocking white lines, containing only a simple, raw, colored shape (e.g., a perfect blue rectangle representing lime acidity).
* **Lighting & Shadows:** There is no "lighting." All shadows are rendered as **dense, uniform, parallel black cross-hatching**, strictly following the isometric grid rules. The shadows communicate immense scale not through darkness, but by the overwhelming repetition and number of the structured lines falling across the cubic city blocks.
