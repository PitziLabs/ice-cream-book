# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism
### **The Yayoi Kusama Edition**

**Style:** An **immersive installation aesthetic** inspired by **Yayoi Kusama**. Every element in the illustration is "obliterated" by an endless, obsessive pattern of **polka dots of varying sizes**. There is no empty space; everything is textured by dots.

**The Vista:** A vast aerial vista of **[City, Country]**, rendered from the immense scale of a **3D satellite map**, but the entire landscape is covered in a **polka-dot pattern**. The street grid and landmarks like **[Landmark 1]** and **[Landmark 2]** appear to be melting into an infinite sea of dots, retaining just enough shape to be recognizable.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, city-sized **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** hovers high in the stratosphere. It is shaped like a giant, organic pumpkin or soft-sculpture and is covered in **dense, obsessive dots**. The "Life-Form" interaction is a single figure peering into a **mirrored hole** in the side of the ingredient, seeing an infinity of dotted reflections.
* **Secondary:** Large **[Remaining Ingredients]** drift at varying altitudes like massive, perfectly spherical "dot-moons."
* **Lighting & Shadows:** Shadows are not realistic; they are composed of **extremely dense, black-on-black polka dots** that pool over the city blocks below, communicating immense scale through pattern density.

**The Palette (National-Culinary Sync):**
* The color scheme is a high-contrast blend of **[Ingredient Colors]** and **[National Colors]**, applied entirely through alternating colored polka dots against solid backgrounds (e.g., Yellow dots on Black, Red dots on White).
* **Atmosphere:** The cloudscape is a dizzying, immersive gradient of floating dots rather than vapor.
* **Geographic Feature:** Iconic features like **[River/Sea]** are rendered as undulating, flowing patterns of densely packed dots.

**The Atmosphere:** Small, stylized **[Local Vehicle Type]** fly among the clouds, shaped like organic gourds and completely covered in dots. **[Texture/Botanical Elements]** drift through the air like perfectly spherical floating orbs.

**The Text:** Integrated into the sky as a physical monument is the phrase: **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"**. The text is written in a handwritten, obsessive script, with **dots filling the counters (the enclosed spaces) of every single letter**.

**The Scale:** **Tiny, ant-like figures and miniature cars** must be visible on the ground within the dotted shadows. These figures are almost camouflaged by their own polka-dot clothing, looking up at the infinite dotted anomalies above to emphasize the cosmic scale.
