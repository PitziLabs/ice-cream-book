# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism
### **The xkcd Edition**

**Style:** A chaotic, hand-drawn, minimalist **black-and-white comic strip** in the distinct style of **xkcd**. This requires **extreme simplicity**, relying entirely on wobbly black lines with zero shading or watercolor. Every character is a simple, identical stick figure (like Cueball, Megan, or the Beret Guy). 

**The Vista:** A sprawling aerial vista of **[City, Country]** that is actually just a **simple, hand-drawn infographic**, rendered from the immense scale of a **3D satellite map drawn with stick figures.** The landscape must be a **geographical simplified sketch**, featuring the grid of streets and landmarks (e.g., **[Landmark 1]** and **[Landmark 2]**).

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, city-sized **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** hovers high in the stratosphere. It is not a physical object, but a **massive, hand-drawn data visualization** or a simple observation. Instead of realistic texture, its side must be **exploded open by a data splatter**, revealing inside a single, highly absurd, minimalist visual gag (the "Life-Form" interaction), possibly related to physics, statistics, or existential dread.
* **Secondary:** Large **[Remaining Ingredients]** drift at varying altitudes like minimal, imperfect, and anxious shapes, drawn with heavy, nervous lines and basic geometry.
* **Lighting & Shadows:** There is no "lighting." Shadows are rendered using simple, wobbly **cross-hatching** or **minimalist splatters** of black dots onto the simplified city blocks below, communicating scale through a frantic visual metaphor.

**The Palette (National-Culinary Sync):**
* The color scheme is **Strictly Black and White.** There is no traditional "National-Culinary Sync." Colors and flavors are implied purely through text and context (e.g., the word "SPICY" is larger and more jagged than "TUNA").
* **Atmosphere:** Raw, scribbled background lines suggest clouds, and the ground grid is simple and flat.
* **Geographic Feature:** Iconic features like **[River/Sea]** are rendered as frantic, hand-drawn hash-marks or simple wavy lines.

**The Atmosphere:** **MULTIPLE** stylized, flying, patched-together **[Local Vehicle Type]** are visible everywhere. These are drawn as unhinged, spiky shapes full of terrified, scrambling stick figure characters. **[Texture/Botanical Elements]** drift through the air like **furious, hand-drawn mathematical scribbles** and exploded seeds.

**The Text:** Integrated into the sky as a **massive, frantic, hand-scrawled 3D monument** or an illuminated cartouche is the phrase: **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"**. The text must be rendered in classic xkcd style: hand-lettered, slightly uneven, sometimes in all caps, and full of parenthetical, pedantic, or dry observations.

**The Scale:** **Countless** tiny, busy, identical stick figure citizens and miniature, ridiculous cars must be visible on the ground within the shadows, all looking up with wide, comedic, minimal eyes, emphasizing the massive scale of the chaotic mathematical nature above them.
