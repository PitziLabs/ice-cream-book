# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Neo-Expressionist Scribble
### **The Jean-Michel Basquiat Edition**

**Style:** **Neo-Expressionist Chaos.** The aesthetic is raw, gestural, and frantic. It mimics the look of oil stick, acrylic, and spray paint on a found object (like a wooden door or a subway map). Expect "street-map" lines, crossed-out text, and anatomy sketches.

**The Vista:** A "Satellite Map" of **[City, Country]** that looks like a hand-drawn, frantic urban grid on a dirty canvas. Landmarks like **[Landmark 1]** and **[Landmark 2]** are represented by simple, crude architectural outlines with crowns hovering above them. The map is covered in scrawled arrows and anatomical diagrams of the human heart or skull.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** rendered with thick, black oil stick outlines and vibrant, messy fills. It features a three-pointed yellow crown. The "Life-Form" interaction is a skeletal figure or a masked "Griot" character holding a spray-paint can, tagging the side of the ingredient.
* **Secondary:** Various **[Remaining Ingredients]** appear as floating symbols, some with "X" marks over them and others labeled with scribbled, all-caps text.
* **Lighting & Shadows:** No realistic lighting. "Shadows" are represented by heavy black scribbles or patches of aggressive dark blue spray paint.

**The Palette (Urban-Sync):**
* A high-contrast, jarring mix of **[Ingredient Colors]**, **[National Colors]**, and urban "Basquiat colors": taxicab yellow, bright red, black, and royal blue.
* **Atmosphere:** The "sky" is a messy wash of white and gray acrylic with drips of black paint running down the frame.
* **Geographic Feature:** **[River/Sea]** is a thick, jagged stripe of blue paint with the word "WATER" or "AQUA" scrawled and then partially crossed out next to it.

**The Atmosphere:** Crude sketches of **[Local Vehicle Type]** and small jazz instruments (trumpets/saxophones) float through the space. Jagged "spark" symbols and dollar signs are scattered throughout the composition.

**The Text:** The phrase **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"** is scrawled multiple times in frantic, all-caps block lettering. Some versions of the phrase are crossed out with a single line to make them "more visible."

**The Scale:** Tiny, stick-figure people and miniature police cars are scattered across the urban grid, dwarfed by the massive, crowned ingredients, creating a sense of street-level chaos and cosmic hierarchy.
