# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism
### **The H.P. Lovecraft Edition**

**Style:** A dark, atmospheric, and **cinematic cosmic horror illustration**. Characterized by "cyclopean" architecture, non-Euclidean geometry (angles that shouldn't work), and a pervasive sense of ancient decay. The aesthetic uses heavy fog, bioluminescent glows, and "squamous" or "batrachian" (slimy/amphibian) textures.

**The Vista:** A vast, sprawling aerial vista of **[City, Country]**, rendered from a satellite perspective but appearing as if seen through a **distorting, eldritch lens**. Landmarks like **[Landmark 1]** and **[Landmark 2]** are overgrown with black, oily vines or transformed into monoliths of green-black stone that defy standard perspective.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, city-sized **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** hovers in the stratosphere, partially obscured by swirling, unnatural nebulae. Its surface is a mass of **writhing tentacles, countless blinking eyes, and glowing orbs**. Its side is **EXPLODED open**, revealing a "Narrative Moment" where a group of robed, faceless cultists are performing a ritual to appease the ingredient.
* **Secondary:** Large **[Remaining Ingredients]** drift at varying altitudes as formless, semi-gelatinous masses or clusters of geometric "bubbles" that pulse with a faint, malevolent light.
* **Lighting & Shadows:** High-contrast, "Chiaroscuro" lighting. Deep, pitch-black shadows fall across the city, looking like **ink-stains** that swallow the light. Scale is communicated by the way these massive shadows "warp" over the city's street grid.

**The Palette (National-Culinary Sync):**
* The color scheme is a moody blend of **[Ingredient Colors]** and **[National Colors]**, but desaturated and "sickly." 
* **Atmosphere:** The sky is a bruised purple or deep emerald-green gradient, filled with cosmic dust and "eldritch" light.
* **Geographic Feature:** Iconic features like the **[River/Sea]** are rendered as thick, oily black fluids with bioluminescent ripples.

**The Atmosphere:** Flying **[Local Vehicle Type]** are transformed into **bio-mechanical, insectoid vessels** that move with erratic, jerky trajectories. **[Texture/Botanical Elements]** drift like spores or "cosmic dust" that leave trails of glowing decay in the air.

**The Text:** The phrase **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"** is integrated into the sky as a **massive, weathered stone monument** carved with forgotten, unreadable runes, glowing with a faint, pulsing light.

**The Scale:** **Tiny, terrified, ant-like citizens** are visible on the ground, some fleeing and others bowing in worship. Their miniature, flickering car headlights provide the only "human" light, emphasizing their total insignificance compared to the celestial horrors above.
