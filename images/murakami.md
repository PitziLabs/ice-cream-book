**Style:** A **"Superflat" anime-influenced style** inspired by **Takashi Murakami**. Characterized by ultra-crisp vector lines, zero depth, and a **manic abundance of smiling flowers** with googly eyes.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, city-sized **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** covered in **thousands of tiny, blinking eyes** and smiling mouths. * **Atmosphere:** Pastel gradients of hyper-saturated hues define the flat sky.
