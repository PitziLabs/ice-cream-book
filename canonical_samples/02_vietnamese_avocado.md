# Vietnamese Avocado Ice Cream
*Absurdly smooth, lightly grassy, and way richer than it looks*

**Difficulty:** CHILL
**Total Time:** 4–6 hours

This one feels like cheating.

Avocado handles fat, texture, and stability without much effort from you. No custard, no temperature management, no real failure points.

The flavor is clean and subtle. Slightly grassy, lightly sweet, and smoother than most traditional bases.

## Ingredients

- 2 ripe avocados
- 1 cup whole milk
- 3/4 cup heavy cream
- 2/3 cup sweetened condensed milk
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

Blend everything until completely smooth.

Taste and adjust sweetness or salt slightly if needed.

Chill thoroughly.

Churn until soft-serve consistency.

Freeze until firm.

## Notes

Ripeness matters. Too firm won’t blend well, too ripe changes the flavor.

Condensed milk provides both sweetness and structure—don’t swap it on your first run.

## What it Tastes Like

Smooth, cool, and clean. Rich without feeling heavy.

Simple, but not boring.
